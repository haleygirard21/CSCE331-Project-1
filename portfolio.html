Portfolio

LTTC Project

In the Summer of 2020, I volunteered to draft a logo design for the Lake Travis Trail Coalition (LTTC), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit that supports the development and maintenance of a safe, sustainable trail network in the Lake Travis area of Austin, Texas that is accessible to all users. I also created homemade physical trail signs for this project and went with a team of local trail-builders and enthusiasts to put them up across our multi-use trail system.


Interning at Ann Shippy MD

During the Summer of 2022, I interned for a Functional Medicine Doctor (see my Qualifications for more information), who used an outdated, bare-boned HTML structure in what she called her patients' nutritional recommendations page; a database program, where each patient's individualized list of test kits and supplements as recommended by the doctor were saved into a private server system, following HIPPA Law regulations.

When the officer manager first introduced me to how to edit this program—which had been previously modified by employees with no true coding experience—was overwhelmed by its lack of organization, structure, and the amount of basic syntactical errors that made me question how the code was even able to function as intended. With my basic background in HTML and coding knowledge from past semesters, I offered to take on the project of debugging and nearly rewriting the entire system to be more user-friendly for the other employees to be able to continue updating in the future. I even consulted with the doctor personally, in order to include any changes that she was hoping to implement into the system and by the end of the summer, I'd done all that they has asked of me, and left with a better sense of understanding in terms of working with HTML, private server systems, and how to collaborate effectively and beneficially in a team environment.

In addition to the above project, I also aided in creating simple graphics and posts for the Doctor's social media pages, including Instagram and Pinterest, as well as for the supplement shop on her website.
